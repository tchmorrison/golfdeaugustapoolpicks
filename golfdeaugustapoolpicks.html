📋 Rules

🎯 Picks (5 per team)

  • 🇺🇸 1 × USA player
  • 🇬🇧 1 × UK & Ireland player
  • 🇪🇺 1 × Continental Europe player
  • 🌍 1 × Rest of World player
  • 🎲 1 × Wildcard (outside Top 50 OWGR)

⚖️ Key Constraint

Maximum 2 players inside the OWGR Top 20.

Rankings based on the Monday before Masters week.

🧮 Scoring

Each player's final score to par. Team score = total of all 5 players. Lowest score wins.

❌ Missed Cut

Any player who misses the cut = +10 penalty added to their score.

🏆 Tie-breaker (in order)

  1. Best individual player score
  2. Most players making the cut
  3. Lowest combined final round score
